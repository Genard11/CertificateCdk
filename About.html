About This Website

This platform is designed to streamline the process of requesting, managing, and tracking certificates. Whether you're submitting a new request, viewing request statuses, or uploading payment confirmations, our system ensures a smooth and user-friendly experience. It integrates with Google Sheets and Google Drive to securely store and access request and payment information, making it efficient for both users and administrators..
